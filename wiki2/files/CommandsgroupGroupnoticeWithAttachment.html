GroupnoticeWithAttachment

http://localhost:8080/group/GroupnoticeWithAttachment/{group}/{attachment}/{token}
Method: Post
OR
GroupnoticeWithAttachment|||{group}~#~{title}~#~{message}~#~{attachment}

Sends a group notice with an attachment

Args helper

NameTypeHint
groupURL argthe UUID of the group
titleTextThe title of the group notice
messageTextThe body of the group notice
attachmentURL argthe UUID of inventory you wish to attach
tokenURL argthe api access token

Possible replys

  • Sending notice with attachment
  • Unknown group
  • Invaild group UUID
  • Invaild inventory UUID
  • Title empty
  • Message empty
  • Missing group Notice power
  • Token not accepted
